# Social Media Creatives

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-09  
**Version:** 1.0

## Overview

This directory contains specifications and guidelines for social media graphics. All creatives follow brand guidelines and are designed for the Vietnamese market.

## Creative Specifications

### Facebook Posts

**Image Sizes:**
- Square: 1200 × 1200px (recommended)
- Vertical: 1200 × 1500px
- Horizontal: 1200 × 628px

**Format:** PNG or JPG
**File Size:** < 1MB (optimized)

### Facebook Cover Photo

**Size:** 1640 × 859px
**Format:** PNG or JPG
**Safe Zone:** Keep important content in center 820 × 312px

### Facebook Profile Picture

**Size:** 400 × 400px (displays as 170 × 170px)
**Format:** PNG (transparent background) or JPG

### Instagram Posts

**Square:** 1080 × 1080px
**Vertical:** 1080 × 1350px
**Horizontal:** 1080 × 566px

### Instagram Stories

**Size:** 1080 × 1920px
**Safe Zone:** Keep content in center 1080 × 1420px

## Design Guidelines

### Brand Colors

**Primary:**
- Deep Navy: #1A2B4A
- Teal: #00A8A8
- Gold: #D4AF37

**Usage:**
- Deep Navy for backgrounds and primary elements
- Teal for accents and CTAs
- Gold for highlights and premium features

### Typography

**Font:** Inter
**Sizes:**
- Headlines: 24-32px (mobile), 36-48px (desktop)
- Body: 16-18px
- Captions: 14px

**Vietnamese Language:**
- Ensure proper diacritics rendering
- Test with common Vietnamese business terms

### Visual Style

**Principles:**
- Simple and clear
- Professional but approachable
- High contrast for readability
- Mobile-first design

**Elements:**
- Use icons and illustrations
- Include screenshots (when relevant)
- Use real business context (anonymized)
- Avoid cluttered designs

## Creative Types

### 1. Feature Highlights

**Template:**
- Icon or screenshot
- Headline (feature name)
- Benefit statement
- CTA button (optional)

**Example Topics:**
- "Quản lý kho hàng đa chi nhánh"
- "Hoạt động offline"
- "Hệ thống khuyến mãi nâng cao"
- "Khuyến nghị thông minh"

### 2. Value Propositions

**Template:**
- Main headline
- Key benefits (3-4 bullets)
- Social proof (optional)
- CTA

**Example Topics:**
- "Đã được chứng minh bởi 18 chi nhánh"
- "Tiết kiệm 5 giờ mỗi tuần"
- "Tăng doanh thu 15%"

### 3. Testimonials

**Template:**
- Customer photo (optional)
- Quote (large, readable)
- Attribution (name, title, company)
- Logo (optional)

### 4. Educational/Infographics

**Template:**
- Title
- Key points (numbered or bulleted)
- Visual elements (icons, charts)
- Branding

**Example Topics:**
- "10 loại khuyến mãi"
- "Mẹo quản lý kho hàng"
- "Lợi ích của hệ thống đa chi nhánh"

### 5. Promotional/CTA

**Template:**
- Prominent headline
- Offer details
- CTA button
- Urgency (optional)

**Example Topics:**
- "Dùng thử miễn phí 14 ngày"
- "Đăng ký xem demo"
- "Early adopter discount"

## File Naming Convention

**Format:** `[platform]-[type]-[topic]-[date].png`

**Examples:**
- `facebook-feature-offline-2025-12-09.png`
- `instagram-value-proven-2025-12-09.png`
- `facebook-cta-free-trial-2025-12-09.png`

## Accessibility

### Requirements
- High contrast text (WCAG 2.1 AA minimum)
- Readable font sizes (minimum 14px)
- Clear, simple language
- Alt text for all images

### Vietnamese Language
- Proper diacritics
- Test readability
- Appropriate business terminology

# Target Personas

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-09  
**Version:** 1.0

## Overview

This document defines the target user personas for marketing and product development. These personas are based on market research and the target audience of multi-location barbershop and beauty chains in Vietnam.

## Primary Personas

### Persona 1: Minh - Multi-Location Chain Owner

**Demographics:**
- **Age:** 35-50 years old
- **Location:** Ho Chi Minh City or Hanoi
- **Occupation:** Owner/Founder of barbershop or beauty chain
- **Business:** 6-20 locations, 3-10 years in business
- **Revenue:** 500M-2B VND/month across all locations
- **Education:** High school or below (some cases under high school)
- **Background:** Started from humble/poor economic circumstances, self-made entrepreneur
- **Tech Comfort:** Low to moderate (uses smartphones, basic apps, but not tech-savvy)

**Background Story:**
- Started with one small shop after working hard to save money
- Grew business through determination, hard work, and business acumen
- Expanded gradually: 1 shop → 2 shops → 5 shops → 10+ shops
- Success built on practical experience, not formal business education
- Smart and capable, but may lack confidence with technology
- Values practical solutions that work, not fancy features

**Psychographics:**
- **Values:** Hard work, determination, practical solutions, proven results, trust, respect
- **Interests:** Growing business, making money, solving real problems, learning from peers
- **Lifestyle:** Very busy, hands-on management, practical and results-focused
- **Decision Style:** Trusts recommendations from peers and trusted sources, values demonstrations over descriptions
- **Risk Tolerance:** Low (prefers proven solutions, needs to see it working before committing)
- **Learning Style:** Hands-on, visual, step-by-step, learns by doing

**Pain Points:**
- **Inventory Chaos:** Losing track of inventory across multiple locations
- **Manual Processes:** Spending 10+ hours/week on manual inventory tracking
- **Lack of Visibility:** Can't see real-time status across all branches
- **Error-Prone:** Frequent inventory discrepancies and manual errors
- **Scaling Challenges:** Current systems don't scale as business grows
- **Offline Issues:** Network problems cause lost sales and data
- **Complex Systems:** Software is too complicated, hard to learn and use
- **Language Barriers:** Technical terms and English interfaces are confusing
- **Lack of Support:** No one to help when stuck or confused
- **Fear of Technology:** Worried about making mistakes, breaking things, losing data

**Goals:**
- **Operational Control:** Complete visibility and control across all locations
- **Time Savings:** Reduce administrative time by 50%+
- **Error Reduction:** Eliminate inventory discrepancies
- **Business Growth:** Scale from current locations to 20+ locations
- **Profitability:** Increase margins through better inventory management
- **Peace of Mind:** Trust that system works reliably
- **Simplicity:** System that's easy to understand and use
- **Confidence:** Feel comfortable using the system without fear
- **Support:** Know that help is available when needed
- **Practical Results:** See real business improvements, not just features

**How We Help:**
- **Simple & Intuitive:** Extremely easy to use, no technical knowledge needed
- **Visual Interface:** Clear icons, simple language, step-by-step guidance
- **Centralized Control:** See all locations in one place, easy to understand
- **Automated Workflows:** System handles complex processes automatically
- **Proven System:** Battle-tested by 18 locations over 3 years (not beta software)
- **Offline Capability:** Never lose a sale, even when network is down
- **Scalable Solution:** Grows with business from 2 to 20+ locations
- **Local Support:** Full Vietnamese language, local support staff, on-site training available
- **Hands-On Training:** Video tutorials, on-site training, ongoing support
- **Peer Success:** See how similar businesses succeeded with our system
- **Value Message:** "The system that powered 18 locations—now available to you. Simple, proven, built for Vietnamese businesses like yours."

**Preferred Channels:**
- **Direct Sales:** Personal meetings, face-to-face demos, referrals from trusted peers
- **Social Media:** Facebook (very active), Zalo (Vietnamese messaging platform)
- **Word of Mouth:** Referrals from other chain owners (most trusted source)
- **Industry Events:** Local barbershop/beauty industry gatherings and events
- **Content Marketing:** Simple video demos, customer success stories, visual guides
- **Peer Learning:** Learning from other successful chain owners

**Communication Preferences:**
- **Language:** Simple, direct Vietnamese (no jargon or technical terms)
- **Format:** Visual demonstrations over written descriptions
- **Style:** Respectful, understanding, patient (not condescending)
- **Content:** Practical examples, real stories, concrete benefits
- **Support:** Prefer phone or in-person support over email

**Quote:**
*"I worked hard to build this business from nothing. I'm not a tech expert, but I know what works for my business. I need a system that's simple, that actually works, and that someone can help me understand. I want to see it working for someone like me before I trust it."*

### Persona 2: Lan - System Administrator

**Demographics:**
- **Age:** 25-40 years old
- **Location:** Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi, or major cities
- **Occupation:** Operations Manager or IT Administrator for chain
- **Business:** Works for chain with 6-20 locations
- **Education:** University degree (business, IT, or related)
- **Tech Comfort:** High (comfortable with software, systems)

**Psychographics:**
- **Values:** Efficiency, organization, data accuracy, system reliability
- **Interests:** Process optimization, technology, analytics
- **Lifestyle:** Detail-oriented, process-focused, problem-solver
- **Decision Style:** Analytical, needs to see how system works
- **Risk Tolerance:** Low (needs reliable, tested solutions)

**Pain Points:**
- **Complex Systems:** Current systems are hard to learn and use
- **Data Inconsistencies:** Inventory data doesn't match across locations
- **Manual Reporting:** Spending hours creating reports manually
- **User Training:** Difficult to train staff on complex systems
- **System Downtime:** Network issues cause system failures
- **Limited Customization:** Can't adapt system to business needs

**Goals:**
- **System Reliability:** 99%+ uptime, no data loss
- **Ease of Use:** System that staff can learn quickly
- **Data Accuracy:** Real-time, accurate data across all locations
- **Efficient Workflows:** Streamlined processes for daily operations
- **Comprehensive Reporting:** Automated reports with insights
- **Support:** Quick response when issues arise

**How We Help:**
- **Intuitive Interface:** Easy to learn, Vietnamese language support
- **Real-Time Sync:** Always up-to-date data across locations
- **Automated Reports:** Pre-built reports, customizable analytics
- **Offline Mode:** System works even when network is down
- **Approval Workflows:** Built-in processes for inventory operations
- **Responsive Support:** Quick help when needed

**Preferred Channels:**
- **Product Demos:** Hands-on demonstrations
- **Documentation:** Comprehensive guides and tutorials
- **Training:** Webinars, video tutorials
- **Support:** Direct support channels (chat, email, phone)
- **Community:** User forums, best practices sharing

**Quote:**
*"I need a system that my team can actually use. Too many systems look good in demos but are impossible to use in real operations."*

### Persona 3: Hùng - Cashier (Thu ngân)

**Demographics:**
- **Age:** 20-35 years old
- **Location:** Any branch location
- **Occupation:** Cashier at barbershop/beauty chain branch
- **Education:** High school to some college
- **Tech Comfort:** Moderate (uses smartphones, basic apps)
- **Daily Tasks:** Process payments, create bills, handle customers

**Psychographics:**
- **Values:** Speed, accuracy, ease of use, job security
- **Interests:** Simple, fast processes, customer service
- **Lifestyle:** Fast-paced work environment, customer-facing
- **Decision Style:** Practical, needs simple solutions
- **Risk Tolerance:** Very low (afraid of making mistakes)

**Pain Points:**
- **Slow Systems:** Software is slow, customers wait
- **Complex Interfaces:** Too many steps, confusing screens
- **Network Issues:** System crashes when network is down
- **Error-Prone:** Easy to make mistakes, hard to correct
- **Training Time:** Takes weeks to learn system
- **Customer Frustration:** Long wait times due to system issues

**Goals:**
- **Fast Processing:** Quick bill creation and payment processing
- **Simple Interface:** Easy to use, minimal training needed
- **Reliability:** System works consistently, no crashes
- **Error Prevention:** System prevents mistakes
- **Customer Satisfaction:** Happy customers, smooth transactions
- **Job Confidence:** Feel confident using the system

**How We Help:**
- **Fast Interface:** Optimized for speed, minimal clicks
- **Simple Design:** Intuitive, easy to learn interface
- **Offline Mode:** Works even when network is down
- **Error Prevention:** Built-in validation, confirmation steps
- **QR Code Scanning:** Quick customer identification
- **Vietnamese Language:** Full Vietnamese interface

**Preferred Channels:**
- **Training:** On-site training, video tutorials
- **Support:** Direct help from support team
- **Peers:** Learning from other cashiers
- **Documentation:** Simple guides, quick reference cards

**Quote:**
*"I just need something that works fast and doesn't break. When customers are waiting, I can't deal with slow or broken software."*

## Posting Guidelines

### Best Practices

**Timing:**
- Morning: 8:00-10:00 (business owners checking)
- Lunch: 12:00-13:00
- Evening: 18:00-20:00

**Frequency:**
- 3-5 posts per week
- Mix of content types
- Don't over-post

**Engagement:**
- Respond to comments within 24 hours
- Use Vietnamese language
- Be helpful and professional
- Share customer success stories

### Content Mix

**Weekly Schedule:**
- Monday: Value proposition / Feature highlight
- Wednesday: Educational / Tips
- Friday: Social proof / Testimonial
- Weekend: CTA / Promotion (if applicable)

## Visual Guidelines

### Image Requirements

- Use brand colors (Deep Navy, Teal, Gold)
- Include Vietnamese text
- Simple, clear visuals
- Professional appearance
- Mobile-friendly (square or vertical)

### Video Guidelines

- Short videos (30-60 seconds)
- Screen recordings for demos
- Vietnamese narration or subtitles
- Clear, simple explanations

This directory contains social media copy for various platforms. All content is in Vietnamese, uses simple language, and focuses on practical benefits for chain owners.

## Content Categories

1. **Value Proposition** - Core benefits and differentiators
2. **Feature Highlights** - Specific features and capabilities
3. **Social Proof** - Testimonials and case studies
4. **Educational** - Tips and best practices
5. **Call-to-Action** - Free trials, demos, consultations

## Posting Guidelines

### Language & Tone
- Vietnamese (primary)
- Simple, direct, helpful
- Professional but approachable
- Action-oriented

### Visual Guidelines
- Brand colors (Deep Navy, Teal, Gold)
- Simple, clear visuals
- Vietnamese text
- Mobile-friendly formats

### Engagement
- Respond within 24 hours
- Use Vietnamese language
- Be helpful and professional
- Share customer success stories

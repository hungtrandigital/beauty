## Financial Strategy

### Funding Requirements

**Total Funding Needed: 2-3 Billion VND (~USD 80k-120k)**

**Breakdown by Category:**

**1. Product Development (40% - 800M-1.2B VND)**
- Technical architecture and infrastructure setup
- Core feature development (6-9 months)
- Mobile app development
- Testing and quality assurance
- Development team (3-5 developers)

**2. Marketing & Customer Acquisition (30% - 600M-900M VND)**
- Brand development and marketing materials
- Website and marketing site
- Initial marketing campaigns
- Sales team and customer acquisition
- Customer onboarding and support
- Target: 30-50 locations in Year 1

**3. Operations & Infrastructure (20% - 400M-600M VND)**
- Cloud infrastructure and hosting (AWS)
- Software licenses and tools
- Office setup or remote work infrastructure
- Legal and compliance
- Insurance and risk management

**4. Reserve & Contingency (10% - 200M-300M VND)**
- Buffer for unexpected costs
- Extended runway if needed
- Opportunity fund for quick wins

### Funding Sources

**Primary Strategy: Bootstrapping + Angel Investment**

**1. Bootstrapping (30-40% of total)**
- **Amount:** 600M-1.2B VND
- **Timeline:** Initial 3-6 months
- **Use:** Early development, MVP, initial infrastructure

**2. Angel Investors (40-50% of total)**
- **Amount:** 800M-1.5B VND
- **Timeline:** Months 3-6 (after MVP/prototype)
- **Use:** Product completion, initial marketing, team expansion
- **Target:** Vietnamese angel investors with SaaS/retail experience

**3. Strategic Partners (10-20% of total)**
- **Amount:** 200M-600M VND
- **Timeline:** Months 6-12
- **Use:** Customer acquisition, market validation
- **Benefit:** Built-in customers, market validation, industry expertise

**4. Grants & Competitions (0-10% of total)**
- **Amount:** 0-300M VND
- **Timeline:** Ongoing
- **Use:** Additional runway, validation, credibility

## Budget Allocation

### Development (800M-1.2B VND)

**Budget Allocation:**
- Team salaries: 600M-900M VND (3-5 developers, 6-9 months)
- Infrastructure: 100M-200M VND (AWS, tools, licenses)
- Testing & QA: 50M-100M VND
- Mobile app: 50M-100M VND

**Timeline:** Months 1-9
**Key Milestones:**
- Month 3: MVP core features complete
- Month 6: Beta launch ready
- Month 9: Feature-complete, ready for public launch

### Marketing (600M-900M VND)

**Budget Allocation:**
- Brand & design: 50M-100M VND
- Website & marketing site: 50M-100M VND
- Content marketing: 100M-200M VND
- Digital advertising: 200M-300M VND
- Events & conferences: 50M-100M VND
- Sales team: 150M-200M VND

**Channel Breakdown:**
- Direct sales: 40% of effort
- Content marketing: 30% of effort
- Digital marketing: 20% of effort
- Partnerships: 10% of effort

**Expected ROI:**
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost): < 5M VND per location
- LTV:CAC Ratio: 3:1 or better
- Payback Period: < 6 months

### Operations (400M-600M VND)

**Budget Allocation:**
- Cloud infrastructure: 200M-400M VND (AWS, scaling)
- Software licenses: 50M-100M VND
- Office/remote setup: 50M-100M VND
- Legal & compliance: 50M-100M VND
- Insurance: 50M-100M VND

**Ongoing Costs:**
- Infrastructure: 4-8M VND/month (Phase 1)
- Software licenses: 1-2M VND/month
- Support tools: 1-2M VND/month

**Scaling Costs:**
- Infrastructure scales with usage
- Target: 15-34M VND/month (Phase 2: 50-500 locations)
- Target: 58-160M VND/month (Phase 3: 500-5,000 locations)

### Reserve (200M-300M VND)

**Contingency Fund:**
- 10% of total budget
- Buffer for unexpected costs
- Extended runway if needed

**Buffer Allocation:**
- Development delays: 100M-150M VND
- Marketing adjustments: 50M-100M VND
- Opportunity fund: 50M-100M VND

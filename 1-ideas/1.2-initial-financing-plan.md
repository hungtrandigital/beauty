## Financing Strategy

### Initial Funding Needs

**Total Initial Capital Required: 2-3 Billion VND (~USD 80k-120k)**

**Breakdown by Category:**

**1. Product Development (40% - 800M-1.2B VND)**
- Technical architecture and infrastructure setup
- Core feature development (inventory, billing, promotions)
- Mobile app development (iOS and Android)
- Testing and quality assurance
- Initial development team (3-5 developers for 6-9 months)

**2. Marketing & Customer Acquisition (30% - 600M-900M VND)**
- Brand development and marketing materials
- Website and marketing site
- Initial marketing campaigns (digital, events, content)
- Sales team and customer acquisition
- Customer onboarding and support setup
- Target: 30-50 locations in Year 1

**3. Operations & Infrastructure (20% - 400M-600M VND)**
- Cloud infrastructure and hosting (AWS/Azure/GCP)
- Software licenses and tools
- Office setup (if needed) or remote work infrastructure
- Legal and compliance (business registration, contracts)
- Insurance and risk management

**4. Reserve & Contingency (10% - 200M-300M VND)**
- Buffer for unexpected costs
- Extended runway if needed
- Opportunity fund for quick wins

### Funding Sources

**Primary Strategy: Bootstrapping + Angel Investment**

**1. Bootstrapping (30-40% of total)**
- **Source:** Founder/team personal investment
- **Amount:** 600M-1.2B VND
- **Timeline:** Initial 3-6 months
- **Use:** Early development, MVP, initial infrastructure

**2. Angel Investors (40-50% of total)**
- **Source:** Vietnamese angel investors, business angels
- **Amount:** 800M-1.5B VND
- **Timeline:** Months 3-6 (after MVP/prototype)
- **Use:** Product completion, initial marketing, team expansion
- **Target:** Investors with experience in SaaS, retail, or Vietnamese market

**3. Strategic Partners (10-20% of total)**
- **Source:** Potential customers (barbershop chains) as early investors
- **Amount:** 200M-600M VND
- **Timeline:** Months 6-12
- **Use:** Customer acquisition, market validation
- **Benefit:** Built-in customers, market validation, industry expertise

**4. Grants & Competitions (0-10% of total)**
- **Source:** Startup competitions, government grants (if available)
- **Amount:** 0-300M VND
- **Timeline:** Ongoing
- **Use:** Additional runway, validation, credibility

### Timeline

**Phase 1: Bootstrap (Months 1-3)**
- **Funding:** 600M-1.2B VND (bootstrapping)
- **Milestones:**
  - Technical architecture complete
  - MVP core features (inventory, billing)
  - Initial team hired
  - Infrastructure setup

**Phase 2: Angel Round (Months 3-6)**
- **Funding:** 800M-1.5B VND (angel investors)
- **Milestones:**
  - Product feature-complete (MVP)
  - Beta testing with 3-5 customers
  - Marketing site and materials ready
  - Initial sales pipeline

**Phase 3: Launch & Growth (Months 6-12)**
- **Funding:** 200M-600M VND (strategic partners, grants)
- **Milestones:**
  - Public launch
  - 10-20 paying customers
  - Product-market fit validation
  - Revenue generation (target: 50M-200M VND/month)

**Key Dates:**
- **Month 3:** MVP ready, begin angel fundraising
- **Month 6:** Beta launch, first paying customers
- **Month 9:** Public launch, marketing campaign
- **Month 12:** 30-50 customers, break-even or profitability

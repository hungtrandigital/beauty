# 1.1 Market Research

## Purpose

This directory contains all market research materials, including raw data, analysis reports, summaries, and reusable templates.

## Directory Structure

- **[reports/](reports/)** - Raw research data, surveys, and detailed reports
- **[summaries.md](summaries.md)** - Executive summaries and key findings
- **[templates/](templates/)** - Reusable research templates for consistent documentation
- **[resources/](resources/)** - Referral sources, PDFs, filtered notes, and links to product specifications

## Usage

1. **Conduct Research** - Use templates in `templates/` to structure your research
2. **Store Raw Data** - Place surveys, raw data, and detailed reports in `reports/`
3. **Summarize Findings** - Document key insights in `summaries.md`
4. **Link to Product** - Reference findings in product specifications (see [domain-specs.md](../../3-technical/3.1-system-foundation/architecture/domain-specs.md))

## Templates

Available templates in `templates/`:
- Research report template
- Survey analysis template
- Competitive analysis template

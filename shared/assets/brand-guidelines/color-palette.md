# Color Palette

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-09  
**Version:** 1.0

## Overview

Our color palette reflects our brand values: professional, trustworthy, efficient, and modern. The colors are designed to work in both digital interfaces (web and mobile) and print materials, with careful consideration for accessibility and the Vietnamese business context.

## Design Philosophy

### Color Strategy

**Professional Trust**
- Deep, stable colors that convey reliability
- Not overly trendy—timeless and professional
- Appropriate for B2B SaaS in the beauty/barbershop industry

**Efficiency & Clarity**
- High contrast for readability
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Accessible color combinations (WCAG 2.1 AA minimum)

**Modern but Grounded**
- Contemporary without being flashy
- Professional without being corporate
- Approachable without being casual

**Vietnamese Market Appropriate**
- Colors that resonate with Vietnamese business culture
- Professional, trustworthy palette
- Works well in both digital and print contexts

## Primary Color Palette

### Primary Brand Color

**Deep Navy Blue**
- **Hex:** `#1A2B4A`
- **RGB:** `rgb(26, 43, 74)`
- **CMYK:** `C: 85% M: 70% Y: 30% K: 50%`
- **Usage:** Primary brand color, headers, key CTAs, navigation

**Why:** Deep navy conveys trust, professionalism, and stability—essential for B2B software. It's sophisticated without being cold, professional without being corporate.

### Primary Accent Color

**Vibrant Teal**
- **Hex:** `#00A8A8`
- **RGB:** `rgb(0, 168, 168)`
- **CMYK:** `C: 85% M: 15% Y: 40% K: 0%`
- **Usage:** Accent elements, links, success states, highlights

**Why:** Teal represents growth, efficiency, and modern technology. It's energetic but professional, distinctive but not overwhelming. Works well for interactive elements and positive feedback.

### Secondary Accent Color

**Warm Gold**
- **Hex:** `#D4AF37`
- **RGB:** `rgb(212, 175, 55)`
- **CMYK:** `C: 15% M: 25% Y: 90% K: 5%`
- **Usage:** Premium features, highlights, special promotions, achievements

**Why:** Gold represents value, premium quality, and success. In Vietnamese business culture, gold is associated with prosperity and achievement. Use sparingly for maximum impact.

## Color Usage Guidelines

### Primary Brand Color (Deep Navy)

**Use for:**
- Main navigation and headers
- Primary CTAs (Call-to-Action buttons)
- Logo and brand elements
- Key section backgrounds
- Important headings

**Don't use for:**
- Large text blocks (too dark, use text colors)
- Error states (use error red)
- Success states (use success green)

### Primary Accent (Teal)

**Use for:**
- Links and interactive elements
- Secondary CTAs
- Success states and confirmations
- Progress indicators
- Active states

**Don't use for:**
- Error messages
- Warning states
- Large background areas (too vibrant)

### Secondary Accent (Gold)

**Use for:**
- Premium features and highlights
- Special promotions
- Achievement badges
- Value indicators
- Limited, high-impact elements

**Don't use for:**
- Primary CTAs (use navy or teal)
- Error or warning states
- Large text blocks
- Overuse (loses impact)

## Accessibility Requirements

### Minimum Contrast Ratios

**WCAG 2.1 AA Standards:**
- **Normal Text:** 4.5:1 contrast ratio minimum
- **Large Text (18pt+):** 3:1 contrast ratio minimum
- **UI Components:** 3:1 contrast ratio minimum

**WCAG 2.1 AAA Standards (Target):**
- **Normal Text:** 7:1 contrast ratio
- **Large Text:** 4.5:1 contrast ratio

### Color Combinations

**Approved Combinations:**
- Deep Navy on White: ✅ 16.5:1 (AAA)
- Teal on White: ✅ 3.1:1 (AA for large text)
- White on Deep Navy: ✅ 16.5:1 (AAA)
- Dark Text on Light Gray: ✅ 12.6:1 (AAA)

**Avoid:**
- Teal on White for small text (use navy)
- Gold on White for text (use navy)
- Light colors on light backgrounds

## Color Psychology

### Why These Colors Work

**Deep Navy Blue:**
- Trust and reliability (essential for B2B)
- Professionalism and stability
- Calm and focused (good for operational software)
- Associated with technology and innovation

**Vibrant Teal:**
- Growth and progress
- Modern and efficient
- Balance between blue (trust) and green (growth)
- Distinctive but professional

**Warm Gold:**
- Value and premium quality
- Success and achievement
- Prosperity (important in Vietnamese culture)
- Attention-grabbing without being aggressive

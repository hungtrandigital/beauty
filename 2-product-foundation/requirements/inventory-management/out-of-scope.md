# Out of Scope: Inventory Management

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-09  
**Version:** 1.0

## Overview

This document explicitly defines what is NOT included in the Inventory Management epic (Epic 1). These items may be considered for future epics or phases.

## Explicitly Out of Scope

### Advanced Inventory Features

**Not Included:**
- Automated reordering based on stock levels
- Supplier management and purchase orders
- Inventory forecasting and demand planning
- ABC analysis and categorization
- Batch/lot tracking
- Expiration date management
- Serial number tracking

**Rationale:** Focus on core inventory management first. Advanced features can be added in future phases.

### Integration Features

**Not Included:**
- Integration with accounting software
- Integration with supplier systems
- Barcode scanning for inventory operations (future)
- RFID tracking
- Third-party warehouse management systems

**Rationale:** Core system must be stable before adding integrations.

### Reporting Features

**Not Included:**
- Advanced inventory analytics
- Inventory turnover reports
- Cost analysis reports
- Profit margin analysis by product
- Custom report builder

**Rationale:** Basic inventory viewing is included. Advanced reporting is in Epic 7 (Reporting & Analytics).

### Automation Features

**Not Included:**
- Automatic low stock alerts to suppliers
- Automatic reorder suggestions
- Automated approval workflows (beyond basic approval)
- Scheduled inventory operations

**Rationale:** Manual control is important for chain owners. Automation can be added later.

## Future Considerations

### Phase 2 Features
- Automated reordering
- Supplier management
- Advanced analytics
- Barcode scanning

### Phase 3 Features
- AI-powered demand forecasting
- Predictive inventory management
- Advanced automation

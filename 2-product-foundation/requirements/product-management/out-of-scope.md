# Out of Scope: Product Management

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-09  
**Version:** 1.0

## Overview

This document explicitly defines what is NOT included in the Product Management epic (Epic 3). These items may be considered for future epics or phases.

## Explicitly Out of Scope

### Advanced Product Features

**Not Included:**
- Product variants (sizes, colors, SKUs)
- Product attributes (material, brand, etc.)
- Product bundling (product packages)
- Product recommendations
- Product reviews/ratings

**Rationale:** Focus on core product management first. Advanced features can be added in Phase 2.

### Supplier Management

**Not Included:**
- Supplier database
- Purchase orders
- Supplier pricing
- Supplier performance tracking

**Rationale:** Not needed for MVP. Can be added in future phase.

### Advanced Catalog Features

**Not Included:**
- Product categories/taxonomy
- Product tags
- Product search with filters
- Product comparison
- Product wishlists

**Rationale:** Basic product management is sufficient. Advanced catalog features can be added later.

### Analytics Features

**Not Included:**
- Product performance analytics
- Best-selling products analysis
- Product profitability analysis
- Sales trends by product

**Rationale:** Basic product viewing is included. Advanced analytics is in Epic 7 (Reporting & Analytics).

## Future Considerations

### Phase 3 Features
